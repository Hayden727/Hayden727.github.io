Diffusion LLMs · SGLang

FDFO — First-Done-First-Out scheduling for dLLM inference

A framework-level execution mode I designed and implemented for diffusion-LLM serving in SGLang. Instead of a batch waiting for its slowest request, each block leaves the moment it finishes — cutting head-of-line blocking for ~1.3–1.45× throughput (PRs #27551, #27877).

About
